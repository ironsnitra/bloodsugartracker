Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2026-01-12

This Privacy Policy explains how Blood Sugar Tracker ("we", "our", or "the App") handles your information.

1. Complete Privacy & Local Storage

We are committed to the principle of "Your Data, Your Device".

  • No Data Collection: We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal health data on our servers.
  • Local Storage: All glucose logs, insulin records, and notes are stored strictly on your device's internal memory using a secure local database.
  • Offline First: The App functions 100% offline. No internet connection is required for core functionality.

2. Permissions

We request only the minimum permissions necessary for the App to function:

  • Storage (Optional): Required only if you choose to export your data (CSV/PDF) or backup/restore your records.
  • Notifications (Optional): Required only if you set up reminders for medication or testing.

3. Data Safety & Sharing

  • No Third-Party Sharing: Since we do not collect your data, we cannot and do not share it with third parties, advertisers, or insurance companies.
  • User-Initiated Sharing: You may voluntarily choose to export your data as a PDF or CSV file to share with your doctor or healthcare provider. This action is fully under your control.

4. Medical Disclaimer

Blood Sugar Tracker is for informational purposes only.

  • The App is a logbook tool and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
  • Never disregard professional medical advice or delay seeking it because of something you have read in this App.
  • In case of a medical emergency, call your local emergency services immediately.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
